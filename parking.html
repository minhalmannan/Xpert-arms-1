Parking Guidance System

The function of the parking guidance system to provide the guideline to drivers for parking into the right place that indicates by the system before getting into the parking area.

LED Display and Indicators

LED Display and LED indicators are used to show in real time free/occupied space for the Parking outside the parking to get into the right parking slot.

Parking Layers and Sectors

Parking layers and sectors can be defined in different categories according to the management plan.

Available Lot & Parking Counter

Each Sector now contains parking spaces and the available lots can be viewed on the display to select specific lane. Once the vehicle is parked, the available lots counter will drop one count.

Sensor & Indicator

The LED indicator is installed the front of each car park space for the convenience of drivers’ observation and inform the available lots to the drivers by its color. It will turn from green to red when it is occupied, or it will keep green. One parking space needs one LED indicator.

Real Time Monitoring

The functionality allows monitoring of real time status of your parking. The specialty of the solution is CCTV synchronization with the parking record all movements as today’s requirements for the security enablement.

Statistical MIS Generation

The system allows management to have statistical data of the traffic flow in the parking slot wise pattern through Monthly/Weekly/daily MIS generation.
